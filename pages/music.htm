music and sound



Theme songs will be provided by the composition genius, Adam Gilbert. Click below to view his website and sample his hit, Otis.

ASCAP award winner Adam Waite recently commented on Adam's rock orchestra piece, Otis.

"Gilbert's skills and talents go well beyond that of today's rock musician; his self penned orchestra arrangement to his song 'Otis' shows a complete understanding of the ensemble. Very few rock groups have been able to achieve what Mr. Gilbert has done here; with his arrangement and composition of 'Otis' he joins the elite company of greats such as The moody Blues and Bjork."
