sky dome



Our sky supports dynamic day and night cycles. We use a look up table where the x value is the time of day and the y value (the columns) determine the sky color from horizon to apex.



These colors are placed onto the vertices of our skydome:




The terrain lighting is done real-time using normals with respect to the direction of the sun light.
